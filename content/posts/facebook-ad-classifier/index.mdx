In this project, I worked with a team of other undergrads to develop a machine learning algorithm to classify political Facebook ads into a variety of categories. We experimented with several methods, including Random Forest and BERT. 
 
Published on the Wesleyan Media Project's Delta Lab website: [Automated Issue Classification of Political Advertisements on Facebook](https://deltalab.research.wesleyan.edu/2021/11/01/automated-issue-classification-of-political-advertisements-on-facebook/)
